Chandausi Pravasi:

Fostering Community

We believe that a strong community is built on shared experiences and a sense of responsibility towards one another. Our initiatives are designed to nurture this sense of community and make it even stronger. Whether it's supporting each other in times of need or collaborating on projects to give back to our beloved small town, we're there for each other. Join us in strengthening the bonds that tie us to our beloved heritage, no matter where in the world we may be.

Featured Project:

Aamodini

Aamodini means ‘Happy Girl’. It is our flagship project with a simple yet profound mission: to identify and nurture the talents hidden within underprivileged families. These are young minds brimming with potential, but often lacking the opportunities and resources to shine. Once identified, these girls become LIGHT (Low-Income Girls with High Talent) fellows. It’s not just financial aid but a holistic approach to empowerment with means to access education, social environment, and mentoring support tailored to each girl's unique abilities.
