Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Chandausi Pravasi ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our website (the "chandausi.org") and make donations through our donation page.

Information We Collect

  • We collect two types of information on our Website:
  • Personal Information: This information can identify you as an individual and may include your name, email address, postal address, phone number, and any other information you voluntarily provide to us when making a donation or contacting us.
  • Non-Personal Information: This information does not identify you as an individual and may include your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URL, pages visited on our Website, and date and time of your visit.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process your donations: We use your personal information to process your donations and send you donation receipts.
  • To communicate with you: We may use your email address to send you information about our organization, upcoming events, and fundraising campaigns.
  • To improve our Website: We use non-personal information to analyze how visitors use our Website and improve its functionality and content.

Sharing Your Information

  • We will not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third party. We may share your personal information with third-party service providers who help us operate our Website and process donations. These service providers are contractually obligated to keep your information confidential and secure.
  • We may also disclose your personal information if required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of ourselves or others.
