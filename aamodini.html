Our Mission

Empowering the Underprivileged

Aamodini means ‘Happy Girl’. This is our flagship project with a simple yet profound mission: to identify and nurture the talents hidden within underprivileged families. These are young minds brimming with potential, but often lacking the opportunities and resources to shine. Once identified, these girls become LIGHT (Low-Income Girls with High Talent) fellows. It’s not just financial aid but a holistic approach to empowerment with means to access education, social environment and mentoring support tailored to each girl's unique abilities.

We have established an Aamodini Professional Development Center situated in Chandausi that offers educational support, tutoring, computer courses, English speaking to underprivileged girls. The center is managed by senior volunteer girls from similar backgrounds so that they understand the needs, aspirations, and challenges of our Aamodini girls. The senior volunteers not only help the girls with their studies but also teach them computer and English-speaking skills. The girls who graduate from our LIGHT fellowship program are then engaged in mentoring junior girls.
